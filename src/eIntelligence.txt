                            Emotional Intelligence
- It is the ability to understand, control and express your feels in a positive way.
- It solves the following
                1. Handles rejection.
                2. Handle stress
                3. Understand and be considerate to others.
                4. Relieve stress
                5. Communicate effectively.
                6. Emphasize with others
                7. Overcome challenges and defuse conflict.

       Aspects of Emotional Intelligence
- Common questions to ask yourself.
    1. What are you feeling?
    2. Do you listen?
    3. Do you control your thoughts?
    4. How do you handle criticism?
    5. Do you show empathy?
    6. How do you treat others?
